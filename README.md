## Project Summary
This report examines how ChatGPT has impacted society in 2024 and 2025. The report looks into how it has been adopted, its benefits, as well as how it poses a risk to society. Its aim is to find out how generative AI has impacted society in relation to decision-making.

# Key Findings
- ChatGPT has worldwide applications in education, business, government, and other sectors of communication.
– Increased productivity, creativity, and accessibility.
Some of the major risks are misinformation, bias, over-reliance on AI, and job displacement.
- International debate on regulating AI is rising.
